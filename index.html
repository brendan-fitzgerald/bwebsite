ABOUT.

Award-winning CG Supervisor and Artist with over 10+ years of experience in Visual Effects across Film, Television, and Commercials. As a CG Supervisor and Artist, Brendan has lead teams on projects such as The Queen's Gambit, Hail, Caesar!, Boardwalk Empire and many more. In addition to being an excellent team player, Brendan is a driven and goal-oriented individual, which has enabled him to successfully complete many projects.
